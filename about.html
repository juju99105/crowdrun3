About Us

We are a community-driven running website that empowers users to discover and share their preferred running routes freely. Our platform offers an accessible database where enthusiasts can search for paths based on location, distance, and difficulty level. Members can contribute their own routes, enriching our collectiveresource. Our service is entirely free, sustained by the generous support of donation and the devotion of our admins.

Why are we doing this?

Promote Accessibility

We believe that everyone should have free access to quality running routes without the barrier of a paywall, which is increasingly common in other running apps.

Foster Community Interaction

Our platform serves as a hub for runners to connect, share experiences, and offer advice, thereby fostering a supportive community of like-minded individuals.

Encourage Outdoor Activity

By providing a diverse range of running paths, we aim to motivate more people to get outdoors and engage in physical activity, which is beneficial for both physical and mental health.

Enhance Exploration Opportunities

We enable runners to explore new and exciting routes that they might not have discovered otherwise, broadening their horizons and enriching their running experience.
